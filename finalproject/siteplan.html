Center Pizza – Site Plan Document

Site Name

Center Pizza – A local pizza shop that serves as a central spot in the neighborhood.

Site Purpose

The website will showcase the photos, contact info, and allow visitors to learn about the pizza shop and how to order.

Scenarios

  • What kinds of pizzas and combos does Center Pizza offer?
  • How do I contact Center Pizza or place an order?

Color Scheme

  • Red: For buttons and headers
  • Green: For highlights and accents

Typography

Fonts: Raleway for headings, Roboto for body text.
