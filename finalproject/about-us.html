About Center Pizza

We are Center Pizza, a beloved local pizza shop with over 30 years of experience crafting delicious flavors. Our menu boasts more than 100 varieties of pizzas, alongside the best empanadas, burgers, and desserts in town. We pride ourselves on being a welcoming, family-friendly spot — and yes, we’re pet friendly too! Whether you’re looking for a casual meal or a place to celebrate with loved ones, Center Pizza is the place to be. Come taste the tradition and passion in every slice!
